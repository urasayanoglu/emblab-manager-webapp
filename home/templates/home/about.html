Welcome to TUAS Embedded Lab Manager

TUAS Embedded Lab Manager is designed to streamline the management of resources within the Embedded Systems Lab at Turku University of Applied Sciences. This application enables students and faculty to easily access lab resources, book lab spaces, and loan equipment for educational purposes.

Core Features

  • Home: Provides quick navigation to other sections of the application.
  • Inventory Management: Track and manage lab equipment, tools, and materials efficiently.
  • Book Tables & Loan Inventory Items: Reserve lab tables and borrow essential equipment for projects or lab work.
  • AI Server: Access AI-powered insights and assistance, integrating lab data for enhanced research and learning experiences.
  • Resources: Discover learning aids and supplementary materials relevant to embedded systems and IoT.

Getting Started

To start using TUAS Embedded Lab Manager, navigate through the main menu at the top. Each section provides specific functionalities:

  1. Visit the Inventory section to browse available lab items, check specifications, notes and location of the item in the lab.
  2. Use Book & Loan to reserve study tables or borrow equipment. Make sure to review the availability status before proceeding.
  3. Access the AI Server for any assistance with lab data, allowing you to query the system and receive AI-powered responses.
  4. Explore Resources for helpful guides, tutorials, and documentation that support your learning in embedded systems.

Support & Feedback

If you have questions or encounter any issues, please reach out through the feedback form available here. We value feedback to improve the application and meet the needs of our staff and students.
